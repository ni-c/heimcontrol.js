RGB Lighting Plugin

With the RGB Lights plugin heimcontrol.js can control analog RGB Lighting strips through a connected arduino.

About

The RGB Lights plugin uses the duino  library to connect to analog RGB lighting strips.

Setup

To connect your RGB LED strip to the Arduino is very simple and requires very few additional parts. For detailed instructions, see Adafruit's  and simply enter which pins it's connected to in the menu.
